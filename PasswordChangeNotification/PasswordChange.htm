You must change your password before it expires.

Methods to change your password:

  • If your computer is connected to the network. *BEST OPTION
    1. Press CTRL-ALT-DEL and select Change Password.
    2. Enter your current password, and then your new password twice.
  • If your computer is not on connected to the company network you can change your password using webmail.
    1. Logon to webmail @ https://outlook.office.com
    2. Click the Profile picture in the upper right corner and select My Account.
    3. In the Password box click - Change Password.
    4. Enter your current password, then your new password twice and click Submit.

Password Requirements:
Always use unique password for your work accounts and keep them separate from your personal accounts.

  • Do make sure your user passwords are at least eight characters long. Twelve or more characters are preferred. Remember your password must contain three of the four: upper case alpha, lower case alpha, symbols or numbers.
  • Do log out of websites and devices when you are finished using them.
  • Don't use a solitary word in any language. Hackers have dictionary-based systems to crack these types of passwords.
  • Don't use a derivative of your name, the name of a family member or the name of a pet. In addition to names, do not use phone numbers, addresses, birthdays or National/Security numbers.
  • Don't use the same password across multiple websites, systems and / or applications.
  • Don't write your passwords down (especially not on a post-it note located on your desk or under your keyboard). Don't share your password with anyone or let anyone see you log into devices or website.

What to do if your password expires:
If your computer is connected to the company network, you will be prompted to change your password when you log on to your computer.

If you are away from the office and your password expires: https://passwordreset.balfourbeattyus.com

After changing your password, you will need to update your mobile devices with the new password.

Updating Your Mail Password on Your iPhone/iPad and Android Device:

All employee should be using the Outlook mobile app to access company email. Outlook mobile will prompt you to enter your password when it has been changed. This can take anywhere from 15-30 minutes after the password has been changed. There is no need to go into your phone's Settings to change your password.
